Who We Are

Oilrise for petroleum services and supply is a young dynamic company founded in 2024. Its 2 core strategies are total quality and flexibility. Any size, any grade, any quality at a competitive price. Adherence to customer delivery needs and complex technical specifications demonstrates our total quality. The unrivalled product portfolio is our core strength, with a strategic location in Cairo.

Oilrise has been established to serve the Egyptian and international markets for the following scope:

  • Supply and service of instrumentation devices for various industries.
  • Maintenance for valves including control, manual, and safety valves.
  • Providing OCTG products and bulk materials.
